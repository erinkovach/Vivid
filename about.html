Product Advantages

Old Technology

No probe had been available that could penetrate cell walls and selectively labels thiols without causing oxidative stress and/or cellular damage.


Probes permanently label the target leading to a permanent change in the conformation of the protein.



Vivid Technology

We offer fluorescent markers that are generated in accordance with our patents. The structure of our compounds can be seen in the figure below. Our products will provide our customers the ability to:


  • Penetrate the cell wall without causing damage to the cell.
  • Are DiThiol based to provide maximum sensitivity.
  • Have low redox potential.
  • Will not permanently bind the target allowing for a more detailed study of cellular function.
  • Co-Localization

Vivid Microscopy, LLC is a company that is focused on the generation of patented fluorescent tag technology that is based in Hartland, Wi. We have secured the patents that cover the technology and have obtained bids for product scale-up. Our company also intends to partner with Concordia University to obtain purity information and for the generation of the certificate of analysis.
