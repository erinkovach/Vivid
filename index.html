What is Vivid Microscopy?

Vivid Microscopy, LLC, based in Hartland, WI, has generated two probes that aid in the understanding of oxidative stress. These probes are available for sale. The technology behind these probes is described in two patents, US7820833 and US8927291. Our company is working with a number of institutions to demonstrate the probes use with a wide variety of applications. also intends to partner with Concordia University to obtain purity information and for the


Patent Number

  • US7820833

  • US8927291

Old Technology

The majority of older probes caused oxidative stress and cellular damage; the very thing they are designed to study.


Probes permanently label the target leading to a permanent change in the conformation of the protein.

Vivid Technology

We offer fluorescent markers that are generated in accordance with our patents. The structure of our compounds can be seen in the figure below. Vivid products will provide our customers the ability to:


  • Penetrate the cell wall without causing damage to the cell..

  • Are DiThiol based to provide maximum sensitivity.

  • Have low redox potential.

  • Will not permanently bind the target allowing for a more detailed study of cellular function.

  • Co-Localization.
