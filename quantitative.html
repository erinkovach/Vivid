Oxidative Stress

This product is used for: The probe is able to measure glutathione in a physiologically relevant range. It can monitor changes in fluorescence emission from both Coumarin and Fluorescein, upon reduction of the disulfide bond that joins this donor-acceptor FRET pair. The ratio of the fluorescence emission band intensity, changes upon reduction with glutathione, and because of the probe’s low reduction potential, permits quantitative measurement of glutathione levels in the 1–10 mM range.
